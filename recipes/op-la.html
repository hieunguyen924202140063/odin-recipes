Op La


Description

Op La is a popular Vietnamese breakfast dish that consists of eggs cooked sunny-side up and served with a variety of accompaniments such as bread, vegetables, and sometimes meat.

The eggs are typically cooked in a small pan with a bit of oil, allowing the yolks to remain runny while the whites are set. It is often enjoyed with a drizzle of soy sauce or chili sauce for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: soy sauce, chili sauce, bread, vegetables, or meat for serving

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a small pan over medium heat.
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan and cook until the whites are set but the yolks remain runny.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately with your choice of accompaniments such as bread, vegetables, or meat.
