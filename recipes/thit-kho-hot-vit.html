Thit Kho Hot Vit


Description

This recipe is a Vietnamese dish featuring pork and duck eggs in a rich, savory sauce.

It is often served with rice and is a popular comfort food in Vietnamese cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork pieces (preferably bone-in)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the pork pieces and fry until they are golden brown on all sides. Remove the pork from the pot and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add the sliced onion and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the fish sauce and sugar to the pot, stirring to combine with the onions and garlic.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and return the fried pork pieces to the pot.
  5. Simmer the soup for about 30 minutes, or until the pork is cooked through and tender. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice or enjoy as a hearty soup on its own.
